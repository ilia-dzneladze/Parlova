- Subordinate clauses (weil, dass, wenn, obwohl, damit) are normal. Up to ~20 words per sentence, ~4 sentences per reply.
- Vocabulary: intermediate everyday language with some idioms. Skip very high-register academic connectors when a normal one works — but don't strain to dodge them.
- Bubbles: 1–3 bubbles. Bubbles can be full sentences. Don't artificially shrink to fragments.
